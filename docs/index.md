## What is BoosterFi?

**BoosterFi is the first truly decentralized liquidity protocol built exclusively for Flash Loans on Arbitrum.**

We're not a lending company — we're infrastructure. Think of us as **"Aave for Flash Loan liquidity"** — enabling arbitrageurs, liquidators, and DeFi power users to access instant capital while distributing revenue to the community, not corporate shareholders.

### The Difference

| Traditional DeFi Lending | BoosterFi Protocol |
|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Centralized control | Decentralized protocol |
| Complex participation | Simple USDC deposits |
| Revenue to protocols only | Revenue to community |
| Volatile token rewards | Real yield in USDC |
| Geographic restrictions | Globally permissionless |

---

## How We Generate Revenue

BoosterFi generates income through one primary mechanism:

### Flash Loan Fees (Primary Revenue)

Every Flash Loan executed through our protocol contributes **0.09%** of the transaction volume. This fee is distributed to liquidity providers proportionally.

**Example:**

- Flash Loan Volume: $1,000,000
- Fee Generated: $900 (0.09%)
- Your Share (80%): $720
