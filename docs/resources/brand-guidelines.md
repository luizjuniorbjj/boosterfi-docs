## Logo

### Primary Logo

Our primary logo features a **pixelated rocket** launching upward, symbolizing:

- :material-trending-up: **Growth** — Boosting your earnings
- :material-lightbulb: **Innovation** — Cutting-edge DeFi technology
- :material-flash: **Speed** — Fast Flash Loan execution
- :material-cube-outline: **Decentralization** — Pixel art represents blockchain's digital nature

The rocket has a distinctive **purple-to-magenta gradient** with pixel particles trailing behind, representing the dynamic movement of capital through Flash Loans.

## Logo Usage Guidelines

!!! success "Do's"
    - :material-check: Use the logo on clean, uncluttered backgrounds
    - :material-check: Maintain minimum clear space around the logo
    - :material-check: Use official color variations only
    - :material-check: Keep the logo proportions intact

!!! danger "Don'ts"
    - :material-close: Don't stretch or distort the logo
    - :material-close: Don't change the logo colors
    - :material-close: Don't add effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
    - :material-close: Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds
    - :material-close: Don't rotate the logo
    - :material-close: Don't use low-resolution versions
